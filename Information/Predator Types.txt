PREDATOR TYPES — Vampire: The Masquerade 5th Edition
======================================================
All 18 canonical predator types, their bonuses, penalties, and choices.


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ALLEYCAT
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You feed by brute force and outright attack, taking blood from whoever you can
when you can. Violence is the quickest route — intimidation makes victims cower,
and Dominate masks the assault.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Celerity (Supernatural speed)
    +1 Potence (Supernatural strength)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Intimidation: Stickups
    Brawl: Grappling

  ADVANTAGES:
    Three dots of Criminal Contacts

  FLAWS:
    Lose one dot of Humanity.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: -1


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
BAGGER
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You consume preserved, defractionated or rancid blood through Iron Gullet —
blood bags, corpses, black-market stock. Maybe you work in a hospital or know
the right people. Ventrue cannot take this style.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Blood Sorcery (Tremere / Banu Haqim only)
    +1 Oblivion (Hecata only)
    +1 Obfuscate (Mental misdirection)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Larceny: Lock Picking
    Streetwise: Black Market

  ADVANTAGES:
    Iron Gullet Merit (•••)

  FLAWS:
    Enemy (••) — someone believes you owe them, or hunts you.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: 0


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
BLOOD LEECH
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You reject mortal blood and feed upon the vitae of other vampires through
hunting those weaker than you, coercion, or taking blood as payment. This
practice is not looked upon kindly.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Celerity (Supernatural speed)
    +1 Protean (Shapeshifting)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Brawl: Kindred
    Stealth: Against Kindred

  ADVANTAGES:
    Blood Potency +1

  FLAWS (choose one):
    Dark Secret: Diablerist (••)
    Shunned (••)
  ADDITIONAL FLAW (automatic):
    Prey Exclusion: Mortals (••)

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: -1


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CLEAVER
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You covertly take blood from close friends and family while maintaining
relationships with them. You go to extreme lengths to keep your condition
secret — or take less pleasant routes.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Dominate (Mental domination)
    +1 Animalism (Control over beasts)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Persuasion: Gaslighting
    Subterfuge: Coverups

  ADVANTAGES:
    Herd (••)

  FLAWS:
    Dark Secret: Cleaver (•) — your human family does not know what you are.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: 0


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CONSENSUALIST
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You never feed against a victim's free will — posing as a charity blood drive,
someone with a blood kink, or even admitting the truth and getting permission.
The last method is a Masquerade breach.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Auspex (Supernatural senses)
    +1 Fortitude (Supernatural resilience)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Medicine: Phlebotomy
    Persuasion: Vessels

  ADVANTAGES:
    Gain one dot of Humanity.

  FLAWS:
    Dark Secret: Masquerade Breacher (•)
    Prey Exclusion: Non-consenting (•)

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: +1


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
EXTORTIONIST
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You acquire blood in exchange for services — protection, security, surveillance.
For as many times as the service is genuine, there are many times when it is
fabricated to make the deal sweeter.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Dominate (Mental domination)
    +1 Potence (Supernatural strength)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Intimidation: Coercion
    Larceny: Security

  ADVANTAGES:
    Three dots split between Contacts and Resources.

  FLAWS:
    Enemy (••) — the police or a victim who escaped and wants revenge.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: 0


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FARMER
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You feed exclusively from animals. The Beast gnaws at you with hunger, but you
have successfully avoided killing mortals except on bad nights. Unavailable to
Ventrue or characters with Blood Potency 3+.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Animalism (Control over beasts)
    +1 Protean (Shapeshifting)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Animal Ken: Specific animal (choose)
    Survival: Hunting

  ADVANTAGES:
    Gain one dot of Humanity.

  FLAWS:
    Farmer (••) — you cannot feed on humans without strong provocation.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: +1


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GRIM REAPER
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You hunt inside hospice care facilities and assisted living homes, feeding from
those near the end of their lives. Constantly on the move to locate new victims,
you may develop a taste for specific diseases.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Auspex (Supernatural senses)
    +1 Oblivion (Command over darkness and death)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Awareness: Death
    Larceny: Forgery

  ADVANTAGES:
    One dot of Allies (Medical community — doctor, nurse, or orderly).
    Gain one dot of Humanity.

  FLAWS:
    Prey Exclusion: Healthy Mortals (•) — you cannot feed from those not near death.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: +1


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GRAVEROBBER
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
Similar to Baggers, you understand there is no good in wasting blood. You dig up
corpses or work in mortuaries — but often prefer feeding from grieving mourners
at gravesites or hospitals.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Fortitude (Supernatural resilience)
    +1 Oblivion (Command over darkness and death)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Occult: Grave Rituals
    Medicine: Cadavers

  ADVANTAGES:
    Iron Gullet (•••), Haven (•)

  FLAWS:
    Obvious Predator (••) — something about you screams death to mortals.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: 0


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
MONTERO
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
Carrying on the aristocratic Spanish tradition of hunting, your retainers drive
victims towards you. This takes the form of long cons, flash mobs, or gang
pursuits — expert planning, patient waiting.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Dominate (Mental domination)
    +1 Obfuscate (Invisibility and misdirection)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Leadership: Hunting Pack
    Stealth: Stakeout

  ADVANTAGES:
    Two dots of Retainers.

  FLAWS:
    Lose one dot of Humanity.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: -1


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
OSIRIS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You are a celebrity, cult leader, musician, writer, or priest. Your followers
come to you willingly, offering blood as tribute. Easy access to blood — but
followers attract their own problems.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Blood Sorcery (Tremere / Banu Haqim only)
    +1 Presence (Supernatural charisma)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Occult: Specific tradition (choose)
    Performance: Specific entertainment field (choose)

  ADVANTAGES:
    Three dots split between Fame and Herd backgrounds.

  FLAWS:
    Two dots split between Enemies and Mythic flaws.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: 0


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PURSUER
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You stalk your victim, learning their habits and routines, determining whether
they will cause an outcry if they disappear. You strike when the time is right
and hunger is at a perfect balance.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Animalism (Control over beasts)
    +1 Auspex (Supernatural senses)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Investigation: Profiling
    Stealth: Shadowing

  ADVANTAGES:
    Bloodhound Merit (•), one dot of Contacts (morally flexible locals).

  FLAWS:
    Lose one dot of Humanity.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: -1


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
ROADSIDE KILLER
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You are always on the move, hunting those who will not be missed alongside the
road. Roadside Killers know the risk is just as worth as the reward — you never
stay in one spot for long.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Fortitude (Supernatural resilience)
    +1 Protean (Shapeshifting)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Survival: The Road
    Investigation: Vampire Cant

  ADVANTAGES:
    Two dots of migrating Herd (across multiple cities).

  FLAWS:
    Prey Exclusion: Locals (•) — you struggle to feed on people with roots in a community.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: 0


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SANDMAN
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You hunt sleeping mortals, gliding through bedrooms like a nightmare. Using
stealth or Disciplines you feed without ever waking your victim — though being
caught means serious problems.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Auspex (Supernatural senses)
    +1 Obfuscate (Invisibility and misdirection)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Medicine: Anesthetics
    Stealth: Break-in

  ADVANTAGES:
    One dot of Resources.

  FLAWS:
    Prey Exclusion: Awake victims (•) — feeding on a conscious person triggers Stains.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: 0


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SCENE QUEEN
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You hunt within a subculture you likely belonged to in life — street culture,
high fashion, underground music. Your victims adore your status and disbelieve
those who suspect the truth.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Dominate (Mental domination)
    +1 Potence (Supernatural strength)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Etiquette: Specific scene (choose)
    Leadership: Specific scene (choose)
    Streetwise: Specific scene (choose)

  ADVANTAGES:
    Fame (•), Contacts (•)

  FLAWS (choose one):
    Disliked (•) — outside your subculture
    Prey Exclusion (•) — a different subculture

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: 0


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SIREN
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You feed while feigning sex or sexual interest, using Disciplines and seduction
to lure prey. Moving through clubs and one-night stands are skills you have
mastered.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Fortitude (Supernatural resilience)
    +1 Presence (Supernatural charisma)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Persuasion: Seduction
    Subterfuge: Seduction

  ADVANTAGES:
    Beautiful Merit (••) [Striking Looks ••]

  FLAWS:
    Enemy (•) — a spurned lover or jealous partner.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: 0


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TITHE COLLECTOR
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
You hold enough power that other Kindred pay tribute in the form of specially
selected vessels, delivered regularly or upon request. The vessels must be kept
in reasonable condition — the Masquerade is everyone else's problem.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Dominate (Mental domination)
    +1 Presence (Supernatural charisma)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Intimidation: Kindred
    Leadership: Kindred

  ADVANTAGES:
    Three dots of Domain or Status.

  FLAWS:
    Adversary (••) — a rival who disputes your claim.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: 0


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TRAPDOOR
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Description:
Much like the spider, you build a nest and lure prey inside — an amusement park,
an abandoned house, an underground club. The victim comes to you. Then you play
with their mind, imprison them, or take a deep drink and send them home.

  DISCIPLINE BONUS (choose one):
    +1 Protean (Shapeshifting)
    +1 Obfuscate (Invisibility and misdirection)

  SPECIALTY (choose one):
    Persuasion: Marketing
    Stealth: Ambushes or Traps

  ADVANTAGES:
    One dot of Haven, plus one dot of Retainers or Herd (or a second Haven dot).

  FLAWS:
    Haven Flaw: Creepy (•) or Haunted (•) — your lair unsettles all who enter.

  HUMANITY MODIFIER: 0
